About Us

About the Institution

Agni College of Technology Chennai was established in the year 2001 by Sri Balaji Charitable and Educational Trust with the objective of producing high quality engineers and technocrats.

The Engineering college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, Affiliated to Anna University Chennai, Accredited by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi and an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Institution.

About the Department

At the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at ACT, our main goal is to empower our students with the technical know-how to analyze intricate problems, design viable solutions and apply their skills to the real world. We foster a culture of lifelong learning, collaboration, and responsibility towards society, so that our graduates are well-equipped to take on global challenges and lead with professional ethics and management skills. As technology continues to advance, we remain committed to producing exceptional professionals who can innovate and adapt to the ever-changing landscape of the field.
